This document records an ontological refinement of Relational Actualism

## Part I: The Ontological Shift

### 1. What RA had been saying

RA's original commitment: **actualization events are primitive.**

This was read as: the actualized causal graph G is the primary reality.
Everything else — superposition, interference, the space of possible
outcomes — derives from G or is defined relative to G. The potentia
(unactualized possibilities) were treated as a secondary structure:
a weighted catalog of what might happen next, generated by G's boundary
and the BDG filter.

This reading is not wrong. But it is incomplete.

### 2. What Berry phase revealed

Six computations attempted to extract Berry phase — an experimentally
observed, path-dependent geometric phase — from RA's native structures:

| Attempt | Probed | Result | Diagnosis |
|---------|--------|--------|-----------|
| Scalar rate modulation | Π (compressed) | γ = 0 | Zero-holonomy theorem |
| Angular depth mixing | Π (compressed) | γ = 0 | Zero-holonomy theorem |
| Boundary-coupled BDG | Π (phases) | γ = 0 | Zero-holonomy theorem |
| Non-actualization amplitude | Π (products) | γ ∝ N | Dynamical, not geometric |
| Causal interval topology | G (BDG scores) | ΔS ≠ 0 at large dx | No orientation |
| Partial order structure | G (interval sizes) | CW = CCW | Reflection-symmetric |

Every attempt that probed G alone or Π alone either gave zero or
gave the wrong kind of phase.

**Berry phase does not live in the actualized graph. It does not live
in the potentia. It lives in their joint evolution.**

This means the actualized graph is not the whole of reality. The
potentia are not a secondary annotation. Both are real. Both are
necessary. And some observables require both.

### 3. What RA must now say

**The possible and the actual are both physically real, but they are
real in different modes.**

This is the central ontological claim of the refined RA.

## Part II: Bimodal Reality

### 4. The two modes

#### The Actual (settled reality)

An entity is actual if it belongs to the actualized graph G.

Properties of the actual:
- **Definite:** it has a specific, determined character
- **Irreversible:** once written, it cannot be unwritten
- **Causal:** it stands in definite causal relations to other actuals
- **Conserved:** it participates in the LLC at every vertex
- **Public:** it is accessible to all future continuations

#### The Potential (unsettled reality)

An entity is potential if it belongs to the potentia structure Π.

Properties of the potential:
- **Admissible:** it is constrained by the actualized graph
- **Structured:** it has internal organization (sectors, adjacency, amplitude weights)
- **Relational:** its character depends on its relations to other potentials and to the actualized boundary
- **Transformable:** it reorganizes under boundary changes
- **Physically operative:** it affects observable outcomes (interference, geometric phase)

### 5. What this is NOT

This is not a claim that "possibilities are real in a Many-Worlds
sense." In Many-Worlds, every possibility is actual in the same mode.
The distinction between branches is perspectival, not ontological.
There is no genuine becoming — only the appearance of becoming from
within a branch.

In RA, the distinction between actual and potential is **ontological,
not perspectival.** An actualized event is settled in a way that a
potential continuation is not. The asymmetry is real, not apparent.
This is why time has a direction, why events are irreversible, and
why the universe produces genuine novelty.

But — and this is the refinement — the potential is not thereby
demoted to mere epistemic bookkeeping. The potential is physically
real. It has structure. It does work. Berry phase proves this.

### 6. The philosophical position

| Framework | Status of the possible | Status of becoming |
|-----------|----------------------|-------------------|
| Copenhagen | Epistemic (not real) | Real but unexplained |
| Many-Worlds | Equally actual (same mode) | Apparent, not fundamental |
| Bohmian | Guided (pilot wave real) | Real, but ad hoc cut |
| GRW/CSL | Real until collapse | Real, stochastic trigger |
| **RA (refined)** | **Real but unsettled** | **Real and fundamental** |

RA is the only framework that makes BOTH the possible and becoming
fully real, without collapsing the distinction between them.

## Part III: The Full RA State

### 7. The universe-state

At stage n, the universe-state is:

**U_n = (G_n, Π_n)**

where:

**G_n** is the actualized ledger:
- A finite directed acyclic graph
- Vertices are actualization events
- Edges are settled causal relations
- Carries conserved quantities via the LLC
- Determines the public causal past

**Π_n** is the potentia structure:
- The structured set of admissible continuations consistent with G_n
- Organized into motif-preserving sectors (σ-labels)
- Equipped with amplitude weights (from the quantum measure)
- Responsive to boundary conditions
- Reorganized by actualization events

### 8. The six axioms

**Axiom 1: Bimodal reality.**
The universe-state is U_n = (G_n, Π_n), where G_n and Π_n are both
physically real but in different ontological modes.

**Axiom 2: Actualization asymmetry.**
G_n is irreversible: once written, an actualized event remains part
of the causal ledger forever.

**Axiom 3: Potentia dependence.**
Π_n is constrained by G_n: only continuations compatible with the
settled ledger are admissible.

**Axiom 4: Feedback of actuality into possibility.**
The transition G_n → G_{n+1} induces a nontrivial update Π_n → Π_{n+1}.
Actualization does not merely read from the potentia; it reshapes them.

**Axiom 5: Public/private distinction.**
Actualized structure is public — accessible to all future continuations.
Potentia structure is not public in that sense, but it is still
physically operative. (This is the RA-native formulation of the
quantum/classical boundary.)

**Axiom 6: Joint observables.**
Some observables depend not only on G_n, and not only on Π_n, but on
the way (G_n, Π_n) jointly evolve along a process. Berry phase is
such an observable.

### 9. The evolution law

The Engine of Becoming now acts on the full state:

**(G_n, Π_n) → (G_{n+1}, Π_{n+1})**

This update has two pieces:

**Step A: Actualization.**
One continuation from Π_n is selected by the BDG filter (S > 0,
ΔS > ΔS*) and written into G_{n+1} = G_n ∪ {new vertex}.

**Step B: Potentia reorganization.**
The act of writing that event changes what is now possible:
- Some continuations disappear (incompatible with the new vertex)
- Some new ones become admissible (enabled by the new vertex)
- Sector relations change (σ-labels may reorganize)
- Interference structure changes (amplitude weights update)

Between actualization events (the adiabatic regime), G is fixed
but Π evolves as boundary conditions change. This is where Berry
phase accumulates.

## Part IV: What This Changes

### 10. The measurement problem (refined)

In the original RA, the measurement problem was dissolved by saying:
actualization writes a new vertex into the graph. The wave function
doesn't collapse; a new fact is produced.

In the refined RA, this becomes sharper: measurement is the
transition of a specific potentia from unsettled mode to settled mode.
The potentia was already real — it was structured, weighted, and
physically operative. Measurement doesn't make it real. Measurement
settles it.

The Schrödinger cat is not "in a superposition of alive and dead."
The cat's vital status is in the potentia — unsettled but structured.
The radioactive decay event settles it. Before the event, the
potentia are organized in a specific way (alive-branch has higher
amplitude for a dense actualization network; dead-branch has lower).
After the event, one branch is inscribed and the other is pruned
from Π.

### 11. The Born rule (refined)

The Born rule — P = |ψ|² — is a statement about the relationship
between the amplitude structure of Π and the probability of
actualization. It is a BRIDGE LAW between the two modes:

The squared modulus of the potentia amplitude for a continuation
equals the probability that continuation will be selected at
actualization.

This is not a postulate about measurement. It is a structural
relationship between the two modes of reality.

### 12. Time (refined)

Time in the original RA was the growing of the graph. In the refined
RA, time is the process by which unsettled reality becomes settled.

Proper time along a worldline is the count of actualization events —
the count of SETTLEMENTS along that path. Time dilation is the
statement that different paths through the same region accumulate
settlements at different rates.

The arrow of time is not explained by entropy or initial conditions.
It is encoded in the asymmetry between the two modes: actualization
is the one-way conversion of potential into actual. You cannot
un-settle a settled fact.

### 13. Berry phase (now placed)

Berry phase is an Axiom 6 observable — it depends on the joint
evolution of (G, Π).

Specifically: when G is fixed (no actualization occurs in the
relevant sector) but boundary conditions cycle, Π reorganizes
through a closed loop. The reorganization is not trivial — the
potentia at the end of the cycle are the "same" (same boundary
conditions, same G) but have been TRANSPORTED through a structured
space. The mismatch between the initial and final potentia
configurations, measured by the next actualization event, is the
Berry phase.

This is why it is geometric: it depends on the SHAPE of the loop
through which Π was transported, not on its duration.

This is why it is observable: the next actualization event (which
probes Π) carries the phase residue.

This is why scalar constructions fail: a single number cannot
represent the structured reorganization of the potentia. The full
sector structure of Π is needed.

### 14. Gauge structure (now placed)

Gauge freedom is the redundancy in how we DESCRIBE the potentia
structure Π. Different descriptions (gauges) of the same physical
Π are related by local transformations of the sector labels.

Gauge curvature is the obstruction to choosing a globally consistent
description. It is measurable — as Berry phase.

Gauge fields are the macroscopic manifestation of the potentia
structure's response to boundary conditions. They live in the Π
sector of the bimodal state, not in the G sector.

This explains why gauge fields are quantum objects (they carry
interference structure) and why gravity is classical in RA
(gravity is sourced by G, the actualized graph, which has no
interference).

## Part VI: The Deepest Sentence

In Relational Actualism, the possible is not unreal. It is the
unsettled but structured part of reality. Actuality is the
irreversible settling of part of that structure into public
causal fact.

Berry phase is the observable proof that the unsettled part of
reality has its own nontrivial organization — an organization
that persists, transforms, and leaves measurable traces even
when nothing is settled.

The universe is not a growing graph. It is a growing graph
entwined with a structured field of possibility, each shaping
the other at every step. The graph is what has happened. The
field is what can happen. And physics is the study of how one
becomes the other.
