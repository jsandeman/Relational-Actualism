# Track A.2 bridge note

`RA_MotifSupportFamilyRescueTaxonomy` is a formal taxonomy layer over the robust positive support-family line. It records that no-worse rescue behavior is licensed by family inclusion/augmentation, not by arbitrary replacement of one support-family construction by another.

The bridge deliberately excludes the v1.x orientation-specific rescue branch. Its purpose is to make the support-family comparison domain explicit for future formal and simulation work.
