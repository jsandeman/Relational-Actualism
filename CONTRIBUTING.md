# Contributing to Relational Actualism (RA)

We welcome contributions, with a particular focus on theoretical physicists, mathematicians, and Lean 4 developers.

2. **The Causal Firewall Limit Theorem:** Formulate a rigorous limit theorem for the threshold $\lambda \cdot \tau_d \cdot l^3 = 1$.
